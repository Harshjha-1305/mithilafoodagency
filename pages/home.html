What is Makhana?

Makhana, also known as Fox Nuts or Lotus Seeds, is an ancient superfood that has been cherished in Indian culture for over 3,000 years. These white, crunchy seeds are harvested from the sacred lotus flowers that bloom in the pristine wetlands of Bihar's Mithila region.
