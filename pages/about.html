Our Story

About Mithila Food Agency

Preserving tradition while nourishing modern lives

Our Heritage

For generations, our family has cultivated makhana in the pristine wetlands of Mithila, preserving traditional methods that ensure the highest quality product while honoring our cultural legacy.

Sustainable Farming

We practice eco-friendly farming techniques that respect the delicate wetland ecosystem while providing sustainable livelihood to local farming communities in Bihar.

Quality Commitment

Every batch of our makhana undergoes rigorous quality checks from harvesting to packaging to ensure you receive only the finest, most nutritious product possible.

Community Impact

By supporting local farmers and preserving traditional practices, we contribute to the economic and cultural sustainability of the Mithila region's farming communities.

Certified Excellence

Our products meet international food safety standards and are processed in certified facilities to maintain the highest levels of hygiene and quality assurance.

Fresh Delivery

We ensure rapid delivery from our processing units to your doorstep, maintaining the freshness and nutritional value of every pack of makhana you receive.
