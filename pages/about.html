Mithila Heritage

From Passion to Promise

My name is Kirti Jha Chaudhary, and I am the founder of Mithila Food Agency. While my professional background is in Banking and Insurance with a B.Com degree, my heart has always belonged to the arts - I am a passionate dancer, painter, and artist. Now, as a housewife turned entrepreneur, I am channeling that creative energy into a new venture born from my roots.

Growing up in Mithila, I was surrounded by our region's beloved traditional snack, Makhana. I watched friends and family enjoy its crunchy goodness, but I was consistently disheartened by the mediocre quality available in the market. I knew something had to change.

This realization sparked my mission: to revolutionize the way people experience Makhana and share its incredible health benefits with the world.

"At Mithila Food Agency, we are dedicated to delivering only the finest, handpicked Makhana, bursting with flavor and rich in nutrients. We believe that healthy snacking should be a delight, not a compromise."

Our goal is not only to make healthy eating accessible and enjoyable but also to promote the rich cultural heritage of Mithila through its most famous superfood.

Join me on this journey as we savor the taste of tradition, innovation, and passion!

About Mithila Food Agency

Preserving tradition while nourishing modern lives

Our Heritage

For generations, our family has cultivated makhana in the pristine wetlands of Mithila, preserving traditional methods that ensure the highest quality product while honoring our cultural legacy.

Sustainable Farming

We practice eco-friendly farming techniques that respect the delicate wetland ecosystem while providing sustainable livelihood to local farming communities in Bihar.

Quality Commitment

Every batch of our makhana undergoes rigorous quality checks from harvesting to packaging to ensure you receive only the finest, most nutritious product possible.

Community Impact

By supporting local farmers and preserving traditional practices, we contribute to the economic and cultural sustainability of the Mithila region's farming communities.

Certified Excellence

Our products meet international food safety standards and are processed in certified facilities to maintain the highest levels of hygiene and quality assurance.

Fresh Delivery

We ensure rapid delivery from our processing units to your doorstep, maintaining the freshness and nutritional value of every pack of makhana you receive.
